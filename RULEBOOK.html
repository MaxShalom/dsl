Summer League Rule Book 2021

  1. Lineups and Replacements
  • Each team has 10 players. 9 + 1 Pitcher
  • If one player cannot come the team may get a replacement but this must be approved by the league board and opposing captain.
  • If no replacement can be found the team can play with 9 forcing them to be shorthanded and have 3 outfielders instead of 4.
  • If more than 5 players cannot make it to the game the team will be forced to forfeit.

  1. General Rules
  • The Ump will flip a coin for who bats first. Winner gets to choose to bat or take the field.
  • 4 Balls and 3 strikes
  • 7 inning games
  • Hit by pitch is a walk (player must attempt to move away from a bad pitch/can’t lean into a pitch)
  • No balks
  • 3 out innings
  • Players MUST RESPECT UMPIRE’S CALLS
  • Infield fly rule

  1. Playoffs
  • All teams (4) make the playoffs seeding is as follows,
  • 1 seed plays 4 seed
  • 2 seed plays 3 seed
  • Tie breakers for determining playoff seeding will be based on the following criteria:

1) total season run differential  

2) run differential vs playoff teams

3) head-to-head run differential

4) coin flip

  • World Series will be best of 3 (first team to win 2 games)

  1. Code of Conduct

  • all players are required to respect each other, their teammates, their opponents, the fans, and the umpires.
  • ejections are at the discretion of the umpire. If a player is ejected in one game, he is not automatically ejected for a second game. If he is ejected in the first game of a double-header, he is eligible to play in the second game.
  • Players should not taunt or mock players on the opposing teams. Communication should be limited to cheering for one’s own team, and mutual respect for all players.
  • instruments, drums, horns, and other foreign objects that make noises are prohibited to be used by players while cheering for their team.
  • cursing other players will not be tolerated.
  • excessive arguments with the umpire, cursing an umpire, or insulting or abusing an umpire, will not be tolerated and may result in suspensions.
  • physical altercations will result in an expulsion of the league.

  1.  Draft
  • Each team will have a captain and a pitcher matched up fairly going into the draft.
  • We will experience an open draft. No players are ‘frozen’ or ‘reserved’ to be on a certain team.
  • Draft will be a live draft at someone's home
  • First round draft picks will be picked out of a hat, and from there on it will be a “snake” draft
  • Everyone will be drafted to a team

  1. Schedule of Games
  • Games will be held on Thursday evenings/Sunday mornings
  • First game takes place @ Truax at approximately 6:00 P.M.
  • Second game takes place @ Truax at 6:00 P.M.

  1. Players Stats
  • All players will be able to view their stats on the Google Sheet we will be sending out
  • They will also be able to view their stats on our website (tbd)
  • Stats will be taken by the team captain each game, and will consist of the following:
  1. At bats
  2. Walks
  3. Strikeouts
  4. Hits
  5. RBI’s
  6. Doubles
  7. Tripples
  8. HR’s
  9. Batting Average
  10. On Base Percentage (OBP)
  11. Slugging (SLG)
  12. On base + slugging (OPS)
  13. Errors
  • Pitchers will also have their own stats for pitching which will consist of the following:
  1. Innings Pitches
  2. Earned Runs
  3. Strikeouts (Swinging/Looking)*
  4. Walks
  5. Hit by pitch
  6. Home Runs given up
  7. ERA
  8. WHIP
  9. Wins and Losses
